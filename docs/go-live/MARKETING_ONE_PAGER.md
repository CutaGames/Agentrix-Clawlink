# Agentrix: The Future of AI-Powered Commerce

Agentrix is the first unified payment protocol designed for the AI Agent economy. We bridge the gap between traditional e-commerce and autonomous AI agents.

## Key Features

### 1. AI-Enhanced Checkout
Our checkout widget isn't just a form; it's an intelligent assistant. It can answer product questions, recommend upsells, and handle complex discount logic in real-time.

### 2. Unified Payment Protocol (X402)
Accept payments from humans and AI agents alike. Our X402 protocol allows autonomous agents to negotiate and execute transactions securely.

### 3. Multi-Chain & Fiat Support
- **Fiat**: Integrated with Stripe and Transak for global credit card and bank transfer support.
- **Crypto**: Native support for EVM (Ethereum, BSC, Polygon) and Solana.
- **On-Ramp**: Seamlessly convert CNY/USD to USDC for instant settlement.

### 4. Merchant Automation
- **Auto-Fulfillment**: Connect your Shopify or WooCommerce store for automatic sync.
- **AI Customer Service**: Let our agents handle 80% of your customer inquiries.
- **Batch Import**: Migrate your entire catalog in seconds via CSV.
