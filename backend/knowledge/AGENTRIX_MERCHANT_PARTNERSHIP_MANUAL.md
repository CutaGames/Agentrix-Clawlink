Traditional e-commerce is built for humans browsing websites. The future belongs to **Agent Commerce**, where AI Agents search, compare, and buy on behalf of consumers. Agentrix is your bridge to this multi-billion dollar AI ecosystem.

## 2. The X402 Protocol: Your Unfair Advantage
The X402 protocol (based on Coinbase standard) is the backbone of Agentrix.
- **Instant Settlement**: No more 3-7 day waiting periods for fiat. Receive USDC instantly upon transaction confirmation.
- **HTTP Native**: Your API can now "require payment" natively. A simple 402 error code can trigger a payment flow for any unauthorized request.
- **Pay-per-Usage**: Sell data points, API calls, or physical items with the same infrastructure.

## 3. Merchant Benefits
- **Global Reach via MCP**: Your products automatically become reachable by users on ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini via our MCP integration.
- **Zero Chargebacks**: Leveraging blockchain finality to eliminate common fraud patterns associated with traditional credit cards.
- **Automated Affiliate Marketing**: Set a commission rate and let thousands of AI Agents act as your global sales team.
- **No-KYC Onboarding**: With Stripe and Crypto-rail options, small merchants can get started without the friction of traditional banking.

## 4. Integration Options
